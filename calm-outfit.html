✨ Calm Crystal Style Guide ✨

Elegant Serenity, Inner Peace 优雅宁静，内在平和

Color Palette 色彩搭配

Main: Lavender, soft gray, ivory 主色：薰衣草紫、柔灰、象牙白
Accent: Silver, deep purple 点缀：银色、深紫色

Key Pieces 核心单品

Silk blouses, cashmere sweaters 丝绸衬衫、羊绒毛衣
Wide-leg pants, wrap dresses 阔腿裤、裹身连衣裙

Accessories 饰品搭配

Amethyst jewelry, silver details 紫水晶饰品、银饰细节
Minimal designs, natural stones 简约设计、天然宝石

Styling Tips 穿搭技巧

  • Choose flowing, comfortable fabrics 选择飘逸舒适的面料
  • Focus on quality over quantity 注重质量胜于数量
  • Keep accessories minimal and elegant 保持配饰简约优雅
  • Layer with light, breathable pieces 轻薄透气的层次搭配

Crystal Styling 水晶搭配

  • Amethyst pendant necklace 紫水晶吊坠项链
  • Moonstone and silver bracelet 月光石配银手链
  • Simple crystal stud earrings 简约水晶耳钉
