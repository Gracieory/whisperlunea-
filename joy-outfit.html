Color Palette 色彩搭配

Main: Soft pink, coral, cream white 主色：柔粉、珊瑚色、奶白色
Accent: Gold, light purple 点缀：金色、淡紫色

Key Pieces 核心单品

Flowy dresses, lace tops 飘逸连衣裙、蕾丝上衣
Pleated skirts, knit cardigans 百褶裙、针织开衫

Accessories 饰品搭配

Rose quartz jewelry, pearl details 粉晶饰品、珍珠装饰
Delicate bows, crystal accents 精致蝴蝶结、水晶点缀

Styling Tips 穿搭技巧

  • Layer soft textures for a romantic look 叠搭柔软质地营造浪漫感
  • Mix patterns with solid colors 花纹与纯色搭配
  • Add feminine details like ruffles 添加荷叶边等甜美细节
  • Balance sweet elements with clean lines 甜美元素与简约线条平衡

Crystal Styling 水晶搭配

  • Delicate crystal earrings 精致水晶耳环
